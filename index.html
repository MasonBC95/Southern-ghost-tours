McDonough, Georgia

Haunted
Ghost Walk

Step into the eerie heart of historic McDonough, Georgia — a town where the past has never fully let go. Our guided ghost walk leads you through the candlelit squares and shadowed alleys of one of Henry County's most storied downtowns, where witnesses have reported apparitions, unexplained sounds, and encounters that defy explanation.

Your guide will share documented accounts of paranormal activity, the dark histories of McDonough's most notable landmarks, and the legends passed down for generations by those who lived — and died — here.

This is not entertainment. This is history.

What to Expect

Tours operate rain or shine. In the event of severe weather, all tickets will be honored at a future date. Comfortable walking shoes are strongly recommended — you'll cover a meaningful stretch of the historic district on foot.

Advance tickets are highly recommended as space is limited on every tour.

McDonough, Georgia

Haunted
Pub Crawl

The spirits of McDonough linger long after closing time — and so will you. Southern Ghost Tours has partnered with several of the square's most notoriously haunted bars and restaurants to bring you an evening that blends paranormal history with genuine Southern hospitality.

As you move from venue to venue, your guide will reveal the stories of those who refused to leave after last call — the dearly departed who still call these establishments home. Between hauntings, take advantage of exclusive drink specials and discounts offered at each stop.

Think ghost stories are just for kids? Think again.

Good to Know

This tour is strictly 21 and older. Valid ID is required at check-in. Alcohol is encouraged but never required.

The City of McDonough permits drinks outside in the dining district using a McDonough carry cup. No drinking is allowed in the center of the square.

Southern Ghost Tours has no affiliation with private establishments and is not responsible for the purchase or consumption of alcoholic beverages.

Conyers, Georgia

Haunted
Ghost Walk

Conyers sits in the heart of a state steeped in eerie legends — and it has its own whispers too. This 90-minute walk through historic downtown Conyers takes you past the old textile mill ruins, down streets where locals have reported shadowy figures, and through areas where temperature drops have been documented and disembodied voices heard.

If you're ever in Conyers late at night and hear footsteps behind you on a quiet street — maybe don't turn around too fast.

Our guide will walk you through the documented history of the paranormal in this remarkable town, separating legend from fact and revealing stories that have never fully been explained.

Questions & Answers

Frequently
Asked Questions

Do I have to purchase tickets in advance?
+
Advance tickets are highly recommended. Space on all tours is limited and reservations will not be held without a ticket.
Where do I park?
+
McDonough There are several street parking spots and lots around the square offering free parking on evenings and weekends. A parking deck is located behind the Court House. There is also a lot on the corner of Hampton Street and Tarpley.

Conyers Several street spots offer free parking around Conyers. There is a lot behind The Boo-Tique, accessible from Elm Street just north of Railroad Avenue.
Where and when do I check in?
+
McDonough Meet at the McDonough Welcome Center, 5 Griffin Street. Please arrive 10–15 minutes before your scheduled tour time.

Conyers Meet at The Boo-Tique, 916 Center Street. Please arrive 10–15 minutes before your scheduled tour time.
Should we tip our guides?
+
If your guide has done a great job, tips are always appreciated. The amount is entirely at your discretion and varies by tour and group size. Gratuity reflects customer appreciation for excellent service.
How long are the tours?
+
Ghost Walks are approximately 90 minutes. Pub Crawls run approximately 2 hours.
What happens in bad weather?
+
Tours operate rain or shine. In the event of severe weather, the tour will be cancelled and all tickets will be issued a rain check for a future date. If you prefer not to attend due to unfavorable weather conditions, we will happily issue a rain check.
Are the tours handicap accessible?
+
If you've navigated the sidewalks and curbs in either historic district, we'd love to have you join us. We cannot guarantee the route is 100% accessible for every situation — please contact us directly so we can better understand your needs and accommodate you.
Can I bring alcohol on the tour?
+
McDonough You may not bring your own alcohol, but you may purchase drinks in a McDonough carry cup from participating establishments. Alcohol is not permitted in the center of the square.

Conyers You may purchase drinks from any establishment while on the ghost walk. The City of Conyers permits drinks outside in the entertainment district.
